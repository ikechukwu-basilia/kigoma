Rooms & Suites

A holistic approach to luxury living, the Kigoma offers 297 curated rooms & suites
that ensure that guests would never want, or need, to leave. Guests are invited to customize their
luxury stay with a selection of the most exquisite room offerings
available, including seven distinct suite categories featuring private and open floor
layouts as well as outdoor terraces and city and garden-view balcony options.

Facilities

Meeting Room

A 20-seater meeting room is on ground at the Kigamo
for your business meetings and client negotiations.

Restaurant

Upscale fine dining restaurant and bar serving a variety
of local, continental and international dishes.

Swimming Pool

An Olympic size pool, two standard size pools and a
kiddies' pool, each offering views of the game reserve.

Conference Hall

Two 150-seater conference halls equipped with smart
monitors for presentations for conferences and meet-ups.

Movie Theater

Three 50-seater movie rooms with standard screens are
available to visitors at Kigamo.

Lounge

Open lounge with a view of the game reserve and a bar
for refreshments. The lounge also features a live band.
